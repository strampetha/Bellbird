App

This Application.

This application is designed for users to live tweet to other park goers in order to let them know about what they have seen within the vicinity.

This can range from animals and fauna to visitors reporting rubbish and dangerous areas due to weather.

This application does not require users to have twitter to see these pop ups but to post you will need one.

How to Use.

Using the left panel you can select how you would like to tweet deciding between just a text tweet and a photo tweet.

These then update on the map once sent for all other users with the app to see to know what you have seen.

The right panel contains information on the application, the park and contact information.

Privacy.

Warning this app will appear to others when you update on it so where you tweet will show to others.
